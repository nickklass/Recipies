Bread and Cheese

Bread and Cheese.A Masterpiece in Cuisine and Typical Food Everywhere

No matter what is going on in the world, we can all count on the simple things. Gravity, although not so simple, we can count on, hugs with your dog, you can count on. Another very important part of out society and our livelihlods is the simple, loved everywhere Meal combination of bread and cheese. Food of the gods. Here we find out what it takes to make it.

Using great quality bread from the supermarket(preferably hops-bread), the best yellow cheese you can find and patience of a distinguished chef, you can put together this masterpiece of a recipe.

Ingredients

  • Hops-Bread. (from Trinidad)
  • Yellow Cheese. (also from Trinidad)

Instructions

Look closely at the bread. Admire it's golden crust. Pull it apart making room for the cheese in the center.

Cut the cheese with precision. Not too thick now because it can overpower the hops. Make sure it is just right.

As you place the cheese in the middle, and you put it in the microwave, you stare into the distance and ponder life's big questions. Whatever it may be, make sure to take it out after 15-20 seconds depending on the microwave. Let cool for just enough time to be able to eat it. Enjoy at least 3 in a sitting.
