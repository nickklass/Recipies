CocaCola and Ice

Ahh. CocaCola and Ices, Typical Drink Everywhere

I wont allow this to take up too much time but the thing is that CocaCola is everywhere and we all love it. I dont know what it is about the drink and if it were only sugar, more people would just put sugar in a glass of water and call it. Pouring it just right is important

The small glass bottle of CocaCola from the shop and some ice cubes can change your life.

Ingredients

  • CocaCola. (froma supermarket)
  • A glass to hold it. (Preferably 'Rocks')
  • ice cubes from the freezer

Instructions

Make sure you are ready

Place the ice in the glass. If you do not have ice, refer to the ingredients and make sure you have everyhing you need. This is important.

As you pour and moke make sure the bubbles dont overflow, be grateful for the pleasure you will soon experience. The thing is, without the ice bouncing on you top lip, the flow would not be the same. Also, as you set down your drink, there is a small layer of water from the ice cube that settles on top of the CocaCola. Enjoy as you pass it and get to the sweet bubbling juice inside. Make sure you close your bottle well and set it upright on the door of the fridge.
